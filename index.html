BreakStijl:

A Modern Art Breakout by Nick Battjes

Story

Inpired by the art of Theo van Doesburg, found of the De Stijl movement championed by Mondrian.

Instructions

Space: start the game, pause, unpause

Left & Right arrows: move the paddle left and right

Break all of the blocks with the ball to win! If the ball goes out the bottom of the screen, you lose!

You can refresh the page or finish a game to see a new procedurally generated board.

Enjoy!
