BreakStijl

Instructions:

Start the game by pressing Space. You may pause and unpause it during the game with Space as well.

Use the left and right arrow keys to move the paddle.

Break all of the blocks with the ball to win! If the ball goes out the bottom of the screen, you lose!

You can refresh the page or finish a game to see a new procedurally generated board, inpired by the art of Theo van Doesburg, found of the De Stijl movement championed by Mondrian.
